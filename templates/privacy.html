Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your email address and password (stored as a secure hash)
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter
  • Usage data: basic information about how you interact with the app, such as pages visited and actions taken

We do not collect payment information, government ID, or any sensitive personal identifiers beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Display your expense history and summaries
  • Improve the app's features and performance based on usage patterns
  • Respond to support requests you initiate

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Your expense data is associated with your account and is not accessible to other users. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us or using the account deletion option in your profile settings.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use a limited number of trusted third-party services to operate — such as hosting providers and analytics tools. These services may process your data on our behalf, but only to the extent necessary to provide the service.

We do not integrate with any advertising networks or data brokers. Any third-party service we use is bound by confidentiality obligations and may not use your data for their own purposes.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses session cookies strictly for authentication — to keep you logged in during a browsing session. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any cookies that persist beyond your session for analytics purposes.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channel. We are committed to addressing any privacy concerns promptly and transparently.
